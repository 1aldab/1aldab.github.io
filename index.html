As of Sep. 2010, I am a Ph.D. student of Computer Engineering, in the Computer Communication Research Group (CCRG) under the supervision of Prof. J. J. Garcia-Luna-Aceves. Prior to that, I was a member of the Experimental Laboratory for Internet Systems and Applications (ELISA) at the University of Calgary, AB, Canada where I received my master's in Computer Science. I completed my undergraduate in Computer Engineering at the Sharif University of Technology, Iran in 2008.
